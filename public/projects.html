SELF WATERING PLANT
Completed

• Created using: Java, MATLAB, Arduino
• A feedback system that analyzes the moisture level of the plant's soil,
and instructs the water resevoir to dispense an appropriate level of water via motor.
• An Analog to Digital Converter translated the voltage level of the moisture sensor into a digital value
that the microcontroller could process.
• Created a manual override that allowed incremental water dispense via an onboard button.
• All information is converted to discrete values and graphed for long-term data monitoring.
